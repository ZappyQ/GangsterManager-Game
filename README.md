# GangsterManager

> *Rise from nothing. Rule everything.*

A dark, text-driven crime simulation built with Python. Start at a homeless camp with $5,000 and nothing but hunger. Build your criminal empire from the ground up.

---

## Features

**Survival stats** — Money, health, hunger, thirst, fatigue, caffeine, wanted level. Neglect any of them and the streets will finish you.

**Gang system** — Create your crew, recruit members, manage your organization. Loyalty is earned, not given.

**Open world** — Large pannable and zoomable city map. Back alleys, pawn shops, hospitals, corner stores. Every location has its own risks and opportunities.

**Illegal activities** — Deal drugs on the street, rob stores, mug civilians.

**Drug dealing network** — Find dealer contacts at the homeless camp and negotiate buys over an in-game chat system. Arrange meetups on the map. Hand out your number to street junkies to build a client base — they text you when they need a fix. Meet them, make the sale, watch your back. Clients can rob you. So can rival gangs.

**Real estate** — Daily rotating catalog of 10 properties. Studios, apartments, houses, warehouses, commercial units. Buy up to 30 properties. All visible on the map.

**Inventory & economy** — Buy, sell, pawn items. Every dollar counts.

**Permadeath** — Die of a bullet, starvation or a rival gang's blade and it's over. No second chances.

**Multiple saves** — Run multiple playthroughs simultaneously.

---

## Tech

- Python + pywebview
- HTML/CSS/JS frontend
- Borderless fullscreen UI
- Zoomable & pannable SVG city map
- Lightweight — no installation required
